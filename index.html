Welcome to Hooked On Sonics and thanks for visiting us on the web.
Hooked On Sonics has been winning over Chicagoland audiences since 2002, with their potent blend of high energy dance and rock.
Comprised of serious, over-the-top players, who refuse to take themselves too seriously, Hooked On Sonic's ability allows them to play all styles with total conviction and energy and at a non-stop pace. Songs you may not have even thought you liked will become your new favorites once you hear them played by Hooked On Sonics, whether it be a song from the 60's or something that came out just yesterday.
Now drop what you are doing and come see us in person!
